Power Rankings

Power rankings are based on Elo rating system with following tweaks:

  • k = 32 for regular season games and for 3rd place game.
  • k = 34 for semi-finals.
  • k = 36 for the grand final.
  • k = 24 for all other play-off games
  • If favourite wins an underdog team who has played less than 10 games (recent joiner of the league) then k is decreased by 8. This means that a strong team cannot steal many easy points from a new team.

The basic principle of Elo rating system is that when an underdog beats a favourite they gain more points than when the favourite beats the underdog. Also, when they draw, the underdog gains points whereas the favourite loses. k marks the significance of the game on the new rating.

P.S. I might still tweak the k values when I plot these on a diagram an notice someone stealing easy points or losing many points perhaps undeservedly. It is subjective. The ratings do not tell which team is the strongest this week, they are more of a trend indicator. If for example a team adds a new player that changes the dynamics, the rating will be adjusted only after the team has played with all other teams.

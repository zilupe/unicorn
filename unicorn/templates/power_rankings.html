Power Rankings

Power rankings are based on Elo rating system with following tweaks:

  • k = 40 for the grand final.
  • k = 36 for semi-finals.
  • k = 34 for 3rd place game.
  • k = 32 for regular season games.
  • k = 28 for other play-off games.
  • If exactly one franchise of the two has played less than 10 games then k is increased by 8.

The basic principle of Elo rating system is that when an underdog beats a favourite they gain more points than when the favourite beats the underdog. Also, when they draw, the underdog gains points whereas the favourite loses. k marks the significance of the game on the new rating.
